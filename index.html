Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important factor to consider when selling a house?
The most important factor to consider when selling a house is the price. You want to set a price that is both competitive and realistic. If you set the price too high, you will likely scare away potential buyers. If you set the price too low, you will leave money on the table.
How long does it typically take to sell a house?
The amount of time it takes to sell a house varies depending on a number of factors, including the market conditions, the condition of the house, and the price. In a hot market, a house can sell in a matter of days. In a cooler market, it may take several weeks or even months.
What should I do to prepare my house for sale?
There are a number of things you can do to prepare your house for sale, including: * Making minor repairs and updates * Deep cleaning the house * Decluttering and staging the house * Hiring a professional photographer to take photos of the house
